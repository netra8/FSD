Asia

Asian cuisine is known for its diverse and flavorful dishes, often featuring rice or noodles, fresh herbs, and a balance of sweet, sour, salty, and umami flavors.

Africa

African cuisine varies by region, emphasizing grains, legumes, and stews. North African cuisine uses aromatic spices, while Sub-Saharan Africa features dishes like jollof rice and injera bread.

Europe

European cuisine is diverse, with each country having unique traditions. Mediterranean countries use olive oil and fresh seafood, while Eastern Europe has hearty dishes.
